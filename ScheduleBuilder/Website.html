About

This application was designed with simplicity and ease of use in mind.

Press "start" above and within a few short steps a fully complete monthly schedule

will be ready to distribute to your employees. Our algorithm garuntees that all your

shifts will be distributed as evenly as possible amongst your employees.

We even provide a section to enter days that employees request off in order prevent

shifts from being scheduled on days that the employee is unable to work.
